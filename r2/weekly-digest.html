This week in Medicaid: 14 meetings, 3 major coverage shifts

Go to the Intelligence Feed →

Here’s what happened across Medicaid P&T committees this week.

Texas

Restricted prior authorization for GLP-1 receptor agonists.

Added a BMI threshold requirement and a mandatory 90-day trial of metformin before coverage. The committee cited cost-per-QALY analysis from a recent ICER report.

New York

Tabled a scheduled vote on a key drug class.

Two committee members requested additional time to review manufacturer submissions. The vote is expected to be rescheduled for next month’s session.

California

Added a new step therapy requirement for a biologics category.

Aligning with CMS guidance issued in Q4. Colorado and Oregon are expected to introduce similar criteria at upcoming meetings.

Coming up next week

Meetings scheduled in FL, OH, WA, MA and 9 other states. Notable agenda items include formulary reviews for immunology and oncology categories.
