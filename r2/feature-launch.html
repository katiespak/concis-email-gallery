A new layer in Concis that uses historical committee behavior to surface which states are most likely to change PA criteria for a given drug class in the next 90 days.

The Problem it Solves

Meeting Intelligence tells you what happened. Coverage Forecast tells you what’s probably coming. Until now, anticipating a criteria change meant manually tracking committee sentiment across dozens of meetings and hoping you caught the signals early enough to act. Coverage Forecast does that work automatically.

What’s New

Coverage Forecast analyzes patterns across every meeting, vote, agenda item, and committee submission in the Concis database — then surfaces a ranked list of states showing elevated likelihood of PA criteria changes for your therapeutic category.

How to Access It

Coverage Forecast is available to all Professional and Enterprise subscribers starting today. Find it under Intelligence → Coverage Forecast in your dashboard.
